I am a researcher in Advanced Technology Lab - Multimedia Division at Samsung Research Institute, Bangalore, India. My work mainly concerns with developing statistical models with better generalization across different high-dimensional datasets, for applications in the domain of computer vision. I obtained my B.Tech degree in Mathematics and Computing from Indian Institute of Technology, Guwahati.

My interests are broadly in the field of statistical and robust machine learning. Currently, we are looking into developing deep hierarchical models non-linear in parameters with maximum margin in the high-dimensional input space and with invariance to rigid-transformation as well as deformation of the data and how well they generalize across different datasets when the parameters are estimated using only a single dataset.

My past work experience has been with Microsoft Research, India, where I was a research intern in the CISL (Cloud and Information Services Lab) division. There, I worked on the problem of issue detection in high dimensional time series data using sparse dictionarly learning, sparse coding and clustering techniques. Please check the projects page for more information.
