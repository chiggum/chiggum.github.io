I am currently working on MS in Computational Mathematics at UCSD. I obtained my B.Tech degree in Mathematics and Computing from Indian Institute of Technology, Guwahati.

For some time, I have been working on problems broadly in the area of differential geometry and harmonic analysis.

These days I devote my time in: (1) Proving that asymmetric contraction preserves spherical convexity, (2) How to efficiently choose eigenvectors of a Laplacian so that they reveal the variables that parameterize the underlying manifold and (3) Embedding high-dimensional data on 2-torus.

Places I have worked before - Amazon 126 Lab, Samsung Research, Microsoft Research, INCF (funded by GSOC). :)
